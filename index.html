About

We are the Breadelicious team from Good Samaritan College in Cabanatuan City, Philippines, and we would like to invite you to taste our food in our gymnasium.

Who will be our customer?

Our target market includes students and teachers at Good Samaritan Colleges, as well as staff and others who will enter the institution during the operation of our business simulation. We chose them as our target market because the majority of students and teachers at this institution choose low-cost meals and snacks.
